Our Story

Locally owned and operated in Summerville, SC

Summerville Junk Removal was founded by Mike Conley, a Summerville local with a passion for helping community members reclaim their spaces. After noticing the need for reliable, affordable junk removal services in the area, Mike decided to establish a company that would provide honest, efficient, and environmentally responsible junk removal solutions.

As a Summerville native, Mike understands the unique needs of our community and is committed to delivering exceptional service to every customer. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted junk removal service throughout Summerville and surrounding areas in South Carolina.

Our Mission

At Summerville Junk Removal, our mission is to provide stress-free junk removal services that exceed customer expectations. We aim to:
